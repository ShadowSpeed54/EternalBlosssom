Biography

Ash Nightingale-Blossom is a 23-year-old half-cat girl from Neo Domino City, born to her parents Lily and Cedar.

She was taught the arcane secrets of magic by her mother and trained in the use of a scythe by her father, alongside her older sister, Rose. Similarly to her mother, Ash has a pair of pink wings that amplify her magic. In Duels, Ash uses her Bloom of Life deck.

In VRAINS, Ash goes by the alias "Virtue".
