Biography

Alicia Celestrixia is a 19-year-old young woman from Novo Velocita City, and the younger sister of Astrid Celestrixia.

In her day-to-day life, Alicia works as a nursing assistant at her local hospital, providing care and support to patients in need, and after hours, she works on writing short stories for her online community.

In combat, she dual-wields her scythe, named "Geodesic Incisor", and her Duel Disk, named "Cyberse Loader", at once, utilising the speed and grace of the scythe together with her "Cynet Link" deck to become an overpowering force in combat.
