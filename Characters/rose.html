Biography

Rose Blossom is a 23-year-old half-cat girl from Heartland, the older sister of Ash Blossom-Shikagita.

Like her younger sister, Rose was taught magic by her mother, Lily, and trained in use of a scythe by her father, Cedar. In Duels, Rose uses either an Adamancipator Fossil or Raidraptors deck.

In VRAINS, Rose goes by the alias "Primrose".
