Biography

Kuriboh, a duel spirit of Yugi Muto. A docile fluffy creature that is an ally of Yugi Muto and the Pharaoh Atem. Despite its cheerful and friendly nature, the duel spirit is quite powerful when he chooses to be. Kuriboh has the ability to multiply himself infinitely and to detonate those clones. Kuriboh does not duel by himself.
