Biography

Liv, the medic of Gray Raven. Despite being the youngest member of Gray Raven, she is still quite skilled and experienced. She is a qualified medic, even before she became a construct. She has a pair of Levi-guns for combat. Liv will not hesitate to do anything to keep the ones she cares about safe, no matter what happens to her. She loves all forms of life, humans, animals and machines.
