Biography

During a particularly intensive training session between Ash and Rose, both sisters unlocked the potential of converging their respective potentials into one autonomous being - which they would name Orchid.

Despite existing as a direct result of the fusion of both Blossom sisters, Orchid is her own unique individual, with her own free will independent of Ash and Rose. When Orchid isn't active, both of her halves remain dormant between the sisters.

Orchid's power level exceeds that of Ash and Rose combined, whilst inheriting the skills, traits and abilities of both sisters.

In Duels, Orchid primarily uses a World Legacy deck, however she is capable of utilising Ash's Aroma Rikka Rose deck or Rose's Adamancipator Fossil or Raidraptors decks.
