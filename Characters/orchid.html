Biography

During a particularly intensive training session between Ash and Rose, both sisters unlocked the potential of converging their respective potentials into one autonomous being - which they would name Orchid.

Despite existing as a direct result of the fusion of both Blossom sisters, Orchid is her own unique individual, with her own free will independent of Ash and Rose. When Orchid isn't active, both of her halves remain dormant between the sisters.

Orchid's power level exceeds that of Ash and Rose combined, whilst inheriting the skills, traits and abilities of both sisters.

In dire or dangerous circumstances, Orchid is able to trigger an Awakened Form—an empowered state in which her power vastly exceeds her previous limitations.

In Awakened Form, Orchid's appearance changes drastically, the half-cat attributes of her body replaced with a fully human appearance.

In combat, Orchid prefers to wield a sword in her Awakened Form, rather than a scythe like Ash and Rose—and by extension Orchid herself—use.

In Duels, Orchid primarily uses a World Legacy deck, however she is capable of utilising Ash's Aroma Rikka Rose deck or Rose's Adamancipator Fossil or Raidraptors decks. She does not Duel in her Awakened Form.
