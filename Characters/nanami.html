Biography

Nanami, the Sagemachina and the leader of the Church of Machina. A very friendly construct that loves humans. Her true origins are unknown. Nanami is also much more human-like than other constructs, even having human emotion herself. As the Sagemachina, Nanami is able to awaken other machines. She can even communicate with the Corrupted and other mechanoids.
